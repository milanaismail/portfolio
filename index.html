About me

Hi there! I'm Mila, a passionate front-end developer with a love for all things creative. With a degree in marketing under my belt and currently in my last year of my second bachelor's in digital experience design, I bring a unique blend of skills and expertise to the table.


From developing websites to crafting compelling branding and graphic design, I thrive on bringing ideas to life through code and design. My journey into front-end development was sparked by a genuine love for the craft. There's something incredibly satisfying about turning a concept into a functional and visually appealing website or application.

Outside of work, you'll often find me pursuing my other passions. Whether I'm hitting the gym for a workout, getting my hands dirty with pottery, or expressing myself through painting, I find joy and inspiration in exploring different forms of creativity.


As for the future, I'm excited to continue expanding my skills, taking on new challenges, and making a positive impact through my work. I believe that with passion, dedication, and a sprinkle of creativity, anything is possible.
